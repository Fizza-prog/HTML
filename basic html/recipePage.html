Simple Pancake Recipe

This is a quick and easy pancake recipe that you can make at home with basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Add flour and sugar to a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk.
  3. Mix everything until smooth.
  4. Cook the batter on a hot pan until golden.
